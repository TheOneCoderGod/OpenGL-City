Assignment 2 SkyPacks (2005 set)

These images are thumbnail previews of panoramic images that were created with Bryce 5, and then converted into 6 square JPEGs suitable for use with the skybox class. Click on any thumbnail to download a ZIP file of 6 images.

You're free to create your own skyboxes (teaching staff cannot assist with this), or use third-party skyboxes (provided you attribute the source).

Bonus - 42 skyboxes from 2004 are also available. Check this page for the links, and details on how the older skyboxes differ from these 2005 versions.

Note - it is not a requirement that you use skyboxes in the assignment.
